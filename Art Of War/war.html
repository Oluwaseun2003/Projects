WHO IS GENERAL SUN TZU?

The GREATEST
MILITARY STRAGIST

The oldest available sources disagree as to where Sun Tzu was
born. The Spring and Autumn Annals and Sima Qian's later
Records of the Grand Historian (Shiji) state that Sun Tzu was
born in Qi. Both sources also agree that Sun Tzu was born in the
late Spring and Autumn period and that he was active as a
general and strategist, serving King Helü of Wu in the late sixth
century BC, beginning around 512 BC. Sun Tzu's victories then
inspired him to write The Art of War. The Art of War was one of
the most widely read military treatises in the subsequent
Warring States period, a time of constant war among seven
ancient Chinese states–Zhao, Qi, Qin, Chu, Han, Wei, and
Yan–who fought to control the vast expanse of fertile territory in
Eastern China.
